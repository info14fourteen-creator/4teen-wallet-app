# 4TEEN Screenshot Captions

Last updated: 2026-05-03

These are clean baseline captions for store screenshots. They are short enough to work as overlay copy or store-upload notes.

## Apple / iPhone set

### Wallet home

- `Your wallet home for the 4TEEN ecosystem`

### Send

- `Send supported assets with wallet-level control`

### Swap

- `Swap assets without leaving the wallet`

### Direct buy

- `Move through direct buy flows from one place`

### Airdrop

- `Track airdrop and protocol-side distribution routes`

### Ambassador

- `Access referral and ambassador tools inside the app`

### Wallet home

- `Wallet home for 4TEEN access`

### Send

### Swap

## Guidance

- Keep text short and product-led
- Avoid tiny legal copy on screenshots
- Do not overload one screen with multiple claims
- Prefer one clear idea per image
